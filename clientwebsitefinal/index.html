WELCOME TO

LeChalet Daycare

We are a trilingual (French, English and Farsi), subsidized daycare center. Our main objective is the learning and development of your child. We welcome children from 0 to 5 years of age. For more information about us, we invite you to browse our website.

Our Services

Open Monday to Friday 6:30 am to 18:00 pm ​

8$/day for children from 18 months to 5 years old ​


Meals

We offer breakfast, lunch and two snacks. Meals are prepared on site. The Le Chalet daycare menu is designed to meet your child's energy needs and includes all the foods required for growth. Meals provide a balanced and healthy diet.

Activities

Here at Le Chalet Daycare we focus on integrating your child with group activities that allow them to engage in social interactions with other children such as coloring, puzzles and many more fun outdoor and indoor activities.
